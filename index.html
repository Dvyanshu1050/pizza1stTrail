Our Heritage

Three Generations of Excellence

Founded in 1952 by Giuseppe Rossi in the heart of Naples, Palatable Pizza has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over seven decades. Our family recipes, passed down through three generations, combine traditional techniques with the finest ingredients.
